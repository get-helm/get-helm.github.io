# HELM — Personal Automation Platform

> You steer the ship, we row.

HELM is a personal AI platform that builds and runs the tools you keep wishing existed — customized to how you work, running 24/7 on a machine you own.

You describe what you want in plain English through Discord. HELM builds it, runs it, and keeps it running.

---

## What it does

- **Morning briefings** — one message before you check your phone, with exactly what matters
- **Custom trackers** — portfolio, tasks, watchlists, anything recurring
- **Automations** — things that happen automatically so you don't have to remember
- **Workspace tools** — purpose-built dashboards for whatever you're focused on

---

## What makes it different

**It's yours.** Your data stays on your machine. Your credentials stay in your password manager. You're not renting time on someone else's system.

**It's proactive.** HELM doesn't wait to be asked. It surfaces things you didn't know you needed and challenges your thinking before you go down the wrong path.

**It's conversational.** No dashboards to learn. No forms to fill out. Just tell it what you need.

---

## What you'll need

- A Mac mini (or any always-on Mac) — this is your HELM server
- A Claude subscription ($20/mo) — the AI engine
- A Discord account (free) — your interface
- A GitHub account (free) — for storing your config
- 1Password or similar password manager (recommended)

**Time to set up:** About 45-60 minutes for a complete install.
